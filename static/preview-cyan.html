Prediction Market Arbitrage Bot
Cross-exchange arbitrage on prediction markets, risk-free by design

Exploits real-time price differences between Kalshi and Polymarket on binary-outcome events (sports, e-sports, and more). When the combined cost of both outcomes across two exchanges falls below $1.00, the bot buys both sides — locking in a guaranteed profit regardless of the result.

The system monitors orderbooks via WebSocket and executes both legs in parallel with sub-second latency. A live dashboard tracks prices, spreads, trade history, and P&L in real time. Configurable thresholds, per-market locking, and auto-settlement detection keep everything hands-off.

No directional exposure — every trade is profitable at the moment of execution.
